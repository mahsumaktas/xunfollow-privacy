# Privacy Policy for X Unfollow Tool

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024

## Overview

X Unfollow Tool is a browser extension that helps users manage their Twitter/X following list. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What we access:
- **Twitter/X Page Content**: The extension reads the content of Twitter/X pages to identify users you follow and whether they follow you back. This data is processed entirely locally in your browser.
- **Local Storage**: We store your preferences (whitelist, language settings, activity logs) in Chrome's local storage on your device only.

### What we DO NOT do:
- We do not send any data to external servers
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `activeTab` | To interact with the current Twitter/X page |
| `storage` | To save your preferences locally |
| `scripting` | To run the unfollow automation script |
| `tabs` | To navigate to the following page if needed |
| `host_permissions` (x.com, twitter.com) | To operate on Twitter/X pages |

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:
- Whitelist usernames
- Selected mode preference
- Language preference
- Activity logs

You can clear this data at any time through the extension's settings or by removing the extension.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
