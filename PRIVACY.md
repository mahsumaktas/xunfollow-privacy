# Privacy Policy for X Unfollow Tool

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Overview

X Unfollow Tool is a browser extension that helps users manage their X (Twitter) following list. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What we access:
- **X Page Content** — The extension reads X pages to identify follow relationships. All processing happens locally in your browser.
- **Local Storage** — Preferences (whitelist, language, logs) are saved in Chrome's local storage on your device only.

### What we DO NOT do:
- Send data to external servers
- Collect personal information
- Track browsing activity
- Share data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking tools

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | Interact with the current X page |
| `storage` | Save preferences locally |
| `tabs` | Navigate to the following page |
| `host_permissions` (x.com, twitter.com) | Operate on X pages |

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally via Chrome's storage API. You can clear it anytime through the extension settings or by removing the extension.
